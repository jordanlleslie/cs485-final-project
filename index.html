Demo: Daemons

  • Try writing in the text editor below!
  • The Synthesizer makes your writing more concise.
  • The Smart Friend makes your writing less jargony and verbose.
  • The Devil's Advocate will suggest counter-arguments to your writing, to help you strengthen your reasoning and address opposing points.
  • The Synthesizer and Smart Friend will make changes to your text, but don't worry- if you don't like their changes, you can undo them!
  • Each daemon will work on the full text or, alternatively, highlight a portion of your text to make more specific changes.
